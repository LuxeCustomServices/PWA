# Marketing Brief — BIZZY

Positioning: An invoicing and payments PWA tailored for solo entrepreneurs through SMBs — simpler, faster, and slightly cheaper than competitors.

Key benefits:

- Quick invoices and recurring billing (Stripe)
- Simple onboarding and clean UX (PWA installable)
- Predictable pricing tiers with free trial

Suggested first assets:

- Landing page copy (headline, features, pricing)
- One-pager PDF for small business outreach
- Email drip: welcome -> onboarding tips -> feature highlights -> upgrade prompt
